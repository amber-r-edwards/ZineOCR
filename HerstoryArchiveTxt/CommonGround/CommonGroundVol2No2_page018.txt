LESLIE ANNE EMICK DEFENSE FUND  

was formed in July 1983. Leslie is  
accused of murder in the death of  
her common-law husband, and this is  
the first case of its kind in New  
York State to use the "battered  
woman's defense". Leslie says she  
killed this man after years of abuse  
and because her life and  

her children had been threatened.  
(See article in CG, September, 1983)  
She is now awaiting trial, and her  
defense will cost at least $30,000.  
You can join the planning of this  
committee by calling Hope at 882-8200  
from 12-6, Mon.-Sat., or by sending  
your tax-deductible donation to CAO  
of Erie County, Leslie Anne Emick  
Fund, 70 Harvard Place, Buffalo, 14209.  
See also calendar in this issue for  
info on the Oct. 20 Cocktail party  
benefit.

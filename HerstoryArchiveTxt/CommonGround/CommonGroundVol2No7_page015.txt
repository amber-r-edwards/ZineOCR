MARCH 30-APRIL 1-15TH NATIONAL CON-  
FERENCE ON WOMEN AND THE LAW will  

be held at the Hotel Bonaventure in  
Los Angeles. The conference theme,  
“Bread and Roses: the Economics of  
Feminism," reflects the conference's  
focus on the overwhelming effects of  
the current economic climate on  
women, lesbians, working class/  
low income women and women with  
disabilities. Lawyers, law students  
and legal workers will join with  
community organizers and social  
service providers to discuss issues  
and outline strategies in over 100  
workshops. Keynote speaker Angela  
Davis and the keynote panel will  
help define problems and strategies.  
California Chief Justice Rose Bird  
will be a guest speaker. Accessible  
facilities and sign language inter-  
pretation available. For registra-  
tion materials or further info,  
write: the 15th National Conference  
on Women and the Law, P.O. Box 7308,  
Glendale, CA, 92035-7308.

Connecticut, is offering a new

ceremony based on symbols associa-  
ted with the particular stage of  

the cycle being honored, combined  
with input from our own feminist  
consciousness and concerns. Informa-  
tion regarding Retreat for Women,  
Inc, can be obtained by writing  
directly to: Retreat for Women,  
Inc., P.O. Box 440, Mamaroneck,  

New York 10543.

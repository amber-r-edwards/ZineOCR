lin Street, corner of Franklin and

COMMUNITY SERVICES PROGRAM is a pro-  
gram sponsored by the Metropolitan  
Community Church serving the lesbian  
and gay community of Buffalo. It  

is designed as a community help  
program for lesbians and gays in  
need. Since its recent formation  
the CSEP has already been able to  
help a number of people in our  
community. It has provided $470.59  
from a Direct Cash Assistance Pro-  
gram Fund for help in paying  

utility and rent bills and other  
emergency expenses. Referrals to  
other agencies have been made,  
donated food and household items  
have been distributed. These  
resources were available because  

of concern in this community. You  
can send a check or a list of items  
you wish to donate to CESP, c/o MCC,  
P.O. Box 114, E. Amherst, N.Y.  
14051. You can drop off non-perish-  
able food in the collection box at  
GLCC or EMMA. Donations are tax  
deductible.

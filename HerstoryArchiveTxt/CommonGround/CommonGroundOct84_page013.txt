newsletter, write: Donna Avada,  
Program Director, P.O. Box 1222,  
Santa Cruz, CA 95061.

EARTH'S DAUGHTERS SPONSORS WOMEN'S  
PLAYWRITING COMPETITION: All women  
writers living in Erie County are  
invited to submit previously unpro-  
duced one-act plays for judging by the  
E.D. Collective. Characters need not  
be limited to female roles although  
Earth's Daughters is seeking plays with  
a strong feminist focus. Manuscripts  
should be typed, with name, address  

and phone number on each page. Include  
an SASE with appropriate postage and  
$2.50 reader's fee (check or money  
order made payable to Earth's Daughters)  
All entrants will receive a ticket  

to the eventual production. The winning  
playwright will receive a $200 award  
and a public reading/performance  
mounted by the E.D. Collective. Dead-  
line is October 15th. Send mss. to  
Earth's Daughters, Box 41, Central  

Park Station, Buffalo, 14215.

FEMINIST COLLECTIVE MEMBERS, past and  
present, are invited to participate in  
a national survey on women's experiences  
in feminist collectives. Though  

many women have been involved in  
feminist collectives during the last  
10-15 years, there is very little  
written about women's experiences in  
these groups. Research is being con-  
ducted by a former collective mem-  
ber interested in writing about the  
particular joys and struggles which  
are common to organizations without  
hierarchies. Please contact Maureen  
Hicks, 1355 Westwood, #207, Los  
Angeles, CA 90024, or call (213)  
820-2515 for more information.

Fight Back! Feminist Resistance to

resistance to patriarchal violence.

Voices in the Night, Women Speaking

Getting Free: A Handbook for Women

McCarthy ($7.95). Comprehensive in
scope, this book discusses the decision
to leave or stay, getting professional
help, being your own counselor, and

Woman's Counsel: A Legal Guide for

rights, obligations and legal procedures
most often encountered by women.

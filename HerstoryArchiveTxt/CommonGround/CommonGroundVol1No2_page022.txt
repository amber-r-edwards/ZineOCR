A RECIPE FROM THE KITCHEN OF THE BEANSTALK
ZUCCHINI NUT MUFFINS

3 cups all purpose flour 
2 cups sugar
1 tsp. Baking Powder 
4 eggs (room temp.)
1 tsp. Baking Soda 
1 cup oil (preferably soybean)
1 tsp. salt 
1 tsp. cinnamon 
1/2 tsp. vanilla
1 cup chopped walnuts

Preheat oven to 350°. Generously grease muffin pans. Sift
flour, Baking Powder, Baking Soda, salt & cinnamon, set
aside. Combine sugar & eggs in large bowl & beat at medium
speed for two minutes. Gradually add oil in a slow steady
stream, mixing constantly 2-3 minutes. Add zucchini & vanilla 
& blend well. Stir in walnuts (& raisins if you like).

Fold in sifted dry ingredients just until batter is evenly
moistened. Do not over mix. Spoon batter into muffin tins
2/3 full, bake 25 minutes or until light brown.

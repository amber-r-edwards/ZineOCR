When he works... and when she works, each may make different impressions:

* The family picture on his desk: . . .
Ah, a solid responsible family man.

* His desk is cluttered: . .
He's obviously a hard worker and a busy man.

* He's talking with coworkers: . . 
He must be discussing the latest deal.

* He's not at his desk:
He must be at a meeting.

* He’s not in the office: . 
He’s meeting customers.

* He's having lunch with the boss:
He's on his way up.

* The boss criticized him:
He'll improve his performance.

* He’s going on a business trip:
It's good for his career.

* He's leaving for a better job:
He recognized a good opportunity.

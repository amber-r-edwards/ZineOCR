ASSERTIVENESS WORKSHOPS are being  
presented this summer by Womanfocus.  
The goal is to provide women with the  
tools to take charge of their lives  
and confront feelings of helplessness  
which frequently are socially rein-  
forced. They deal with: resolving  
conflicts constructively; expressing  
anger, giving and receiving criticism,  
asking for what you want and saying  
no. Learning will be active, role  
playing and experiential exercises  
used.

TELEPHONE ASSURANCE PROGRAM is a free,  
organized volunteer service which  
calls elderly, handicapped, chron-  
ically or temporarily ill persons  
who live alone. Its basic purpose is  
to help satisfy the desire of people  
to live independently by eliminating  
some of the fears and dangers that  
living alone involves. Regular  
telephone calls are made at a sche-  
duled time, agreed upon by the  
caller and the recipient. For  
further information on receiving  
calls or becoming a volunteer caller,  
contact 887-2625.

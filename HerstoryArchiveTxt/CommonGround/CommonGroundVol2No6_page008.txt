The "Women Helping Women Awards"  
for 1983 were presented at the  
December Buffalo N.O.W. meeting.  

Those who received awards were  
Myrna Ford, Executive Director of  
Everywoman Opportunity Center and  
Program Director of the Displaced  
Homemaker Center (top left), Common  
Ground (some shining faces top right),  
the Holiday Inn 10, Shirley Rock  
Antinarelli and Clara McDonnell ac-  
cepting the award (not pictured) and  
Sharon Hladczuk (bottom right) who  
is representing herself in a child  
support suit against her ex-husband.  
Kathy Jackson was also recognized for  
her activities in N.O.W. (pictured  
bottom left).  

Congratulations all!!

Peace Education for Women Project  

Some WAND members are beginning a new effort in peace education.  

We propose to  

provide a handbook which will include 12 peace activities for 12 monthly meetings  
and take it to clubs, church, neighborhood, union organizations - wherever women  

are already organized.  

This will be a good source of education for those who work  

on the project as well. Angeliki Keil is planning the project and will call you  
if you indicate your interest on the enclosed form.

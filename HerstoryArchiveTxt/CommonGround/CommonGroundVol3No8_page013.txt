THE CINEMA GUILD has three new re-  
leases (for rent and sale - 16 mm and  
video) of particular interest to  
women: Abortion: Stories from the  
North and South is an award-winning  
documentary. It is a factual, cross  
cultural survey of many women's  
lives, intended to provoke further  
discussion of the issue. Rape/Crisis  
is an award-winning documentary on  
the roots of sexual violence in our  
culture. It concentrates on the  
aftermath of rape, the crisis for  
everyone concerned and the work of  

a rape crisis center. We Dig Coal  

is a portrait of three women who  
fought a sexual discrimination suit  
to get their jobs as coal miners.  
One, Marilyn McCusker, was the  

first woman to die in a deep mine  

in the U.S. For further information  
or a complete catalog, write to  

The Cinema Guild, 1697 Broadway;  
New York, N.Y. 10019.

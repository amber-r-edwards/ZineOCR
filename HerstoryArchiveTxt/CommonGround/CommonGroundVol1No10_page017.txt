THE BUFFALO WOMEN'S LIBERATION  
UNION has reorganized as a support/  
study group for Socialist Feminist  
activist women. "The lack of women  
power and the increasing pressures  
we experience in these difficult  
times brought us to a re-examina-  
tion of our goals and resulted in  
a change in our organization.  

Women in the new group are involved  
in a variety of projects...As a  
group, we will sponsor at least one  
event a year, probably an educa-  
tional outreach event for the  
annual celebration of International  
Women's Day. Unlike the former  
B.W.L.U., the new group will no  
longer send representatives to  
activist organizations in the city.  
Members will be active in various  
groups as individuals, rather than  
official B.W.L.U. Also we have  
discontinued our bank account and  
P.O. Box.” The Buffalo Women's  
Liberation Union welcomes other  
progressive women. Anyone inter-  
ested in participating in the  
group can call 881-5585.

NOW IS THE TIME

Please join NOW by filling out this
coupon and mailing it to: NOW Membership,
31 Hodge Ave., Buffalo 14222.

Dues are $45 to $15 a year. If you
cannot afford the $45, you can choose
an amount between the two figures
that you feel you can afford. (If
you can give more, please do.) Membership
in National, State and local
NOW is included in the $45 to $15
fee, and it entitles you to a year's
subscription to the National NOW
Times, State newsletter and Buffalo
NOW's Feminist Observer.

If you do not wish to become a
member of National, State and local
NOW at this particular time, please
join Buffalo NOW as an Associate
Member (non-voting) for $5.00 a
year. This membership entitles
you to a year's subscription to
our publication.

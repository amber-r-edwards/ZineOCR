Do you want to meet women in the community? Find out what organizations  
and resources are available in the Buffalo area? Get involved in the local  
women's community? Make contacts with other agencies working toward goals  

similar to yours?  

Yes, you can do all this, as well as enjoy a wonderful party and  
celebrate Common Ground's second anniversary, at one of the social  
events of the season, The Buffalo Women's Tea Party!  

Sunday, September 30th, at 7 p.m. is the kick-off of this  
reception, sponsored by Common Ground, The Western New York  
Women's Newsjournal, and EMMA, The Western New York Women's  
Resource and Cultural Center at the Downtown YWCA, 190  
Franklin Street.  

Women from a variety of local organizations will be  
there to answer your questions. Refreshments will be  
provided. (The reception is free, but the donation  
cans will also be present!) Mingling, networking  
and partying are the themes of the evening, as  

well as the chance for EMMA and Common Ground  

to tell you more about the operations of our  
volunteer collectives and show you how you  

might become involved.  

This is not only a reception for  
any interested individual in the  
women's community, but a chance for  

women to meet their counterparts  

in other local agencies and  
organizations.  

For more information, call EMMA  
at 885-2285. If your organization  
is interested in having a representative  
and/or display at the reception and we have not yet contacted you, please call us! We'd love to hear  
from you, and we are looking forward to a good time, productive evening and a large crowd!

RELIGIOUS GROUPS  

WOMEN GLOW is a Christian organiza-  
tion devoted to drawing women closer  
to God—to have a personal relation-  
ship with Him on their walk through  
life—to bring glory and honor and  
praise to our Lord Jesus Christ.  
Women of all ages, colors and creeds  
attend.  

Meetings are the first Saturday  
of every month, 9 A.M. at the Air-  
port Holiday Inn on Genesee Street.  
Buffet breakfast followed by prayer  
requests and singing. Women minister  
to each other, those who wish to be  
prayed with are ministered to by the  
elders.

GAY LAW STUDENTS ORGANIZATION was  
chartered in the Spring of 1982 at  
UB. The group is dedicated to  
the extension of full civil rights  
for all persons, regardless of  
sexual orientation. A legal resource  
center is being established to deal  
with lesbian/gay concerns—including  
housing and employment discrimination,  
custody, immigration and military  
policies. GLSO serves as a liaison  
between the legal and gay communities  
of Buffalo, providing speakers,  
films, referrals and general information.  
GLSO is located at 118 O'Brian Hall,  
SUNY, Buffalo, 636-2161.

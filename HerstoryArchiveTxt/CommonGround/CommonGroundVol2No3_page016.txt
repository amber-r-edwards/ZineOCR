NOV. 16 - BUFFALO N.O.W. MEETING will  
include the film “How Women Got the  
Vote" narrated by Jean Stapleton. It  
is the saga of how women achieved  
suffragism in an epic of determined,  

organized willpower, fighting husbands,  

clergymen, senators, presidents and a  
set of social traditions that went  
back generations. It features popu-  
lar songs of the times lampooning  
women's rights. There are voices of  
pioneer women in the movement, in-  
cluding Alice Paul, who helped form  
the National Women's Party in 1916.  
Visuals are accompanied by newsreels  
and cartoons as well as excerpts from  
feature films depicting women in the  
movement, some ridiculing their  
aspirations.

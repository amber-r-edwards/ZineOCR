Palestinian Refugees in South Lebanon

The following photos of Palestinian refugees were taken in South Lebanon after the
Israeli invasion during the summer of 1982, for the United Nations Relief and
Works Agency. Between Our Selves remembers the three-year anniversary of the
invasion.

suppose there was a wimmin of color  
music & arts festival in the u.s.a?

where will it be?

will it be indoors? outdoors? in-  
doors & outdoors? how long will it  
last? 1 day? 3 days? a week? longer?  
will it be held during summer? will it  
be in the city? in the woods?

who will attend?

lesbians? heterosexual wimmin? gay  
men? any man accompanied by a  
woman? people of color only?  
anybody who has the price of admis-  
sion? wimmin only? will mothers  
bring female & male children? what  
child care services will be available?  
will wimmin from other parts of the  
world participate? asia? africa? latin  
america? the middle east? the pacific?  
the caribbean? europe?

how much will it cost to get in?

will there be discounts for seniors,  
kids, students, unemployed, welfare?  
will there be package deals including  
roundtrip transportation/festival entry/  
lodging/food?

how much will the festival cost to  
produce?

where will the money come from?  
how will we find each other & come  
together in a reasonably representative  
organizing group with the skill/will to  
see this festival through? how long  
will it take to do fundraising and  
organizing? 6 months? 1 year? 2 years?  
how will we define wimmin of color?  
how will we spell ourselves: women?  
wimmin? will kids, seniors & the  
otherwise able be involved in the plan-  
ning? what kinds of help will we  
accept from white wimmin? men of  
color? white men? whose money will  
we refuse to accept? when & how will  
we get started?

who will be the featured artists?  
wimmin only? feminists only? les-  
bians? heterosexual wimmin? the  
otherwise able? kids? seniors? groups  
led by wimmin of color that include  
white wimmin? men of color? white  
men? how much effort/time will be  
put into scouting artists not already  
known on the wimmins circuit? will  
there be folk-traditional artists? pop-  
oriented artists? what about euro-  
classical artists? (some of us are into  
that too.) will there be comedy?  
poetry? mime? film? theatre?  
clowns? puppets? video? photo exhi-  
bits? art exhibits? musicmusicmusic?  
will there be an array of performing &  
visual artists from all our cultures?  
will all of our languages & symbols &  
signs & sounds be represented? name  
5 wimmin performing artists or visual  
artists from cultures other than your  
own? how often do you listen to  
songs sung in a language you don’t  
know?

what kinds of foods will be available?

vegetables only? fruit? non-dairy?  
raw foods? spare ribs? an interna-  
tional variety of fried fish & chicken?

bar-b-que tofu? will there be a de-  
lectable variety of fat-sugar-salt-m.s.g.-  
preservative-free foods & beverages?  
will there be wine & beer? fresh-  
squeezed juices? soda? herbs? what  
about sweets? how much will a meal  
cost? how comfortable are you eating  
foods from cultures other than your  
own?

will we do a couple of mini-festivals  
first to iron out the problems?

or will we plunge right into the biggie?  
how many areas of activity will there  
be? will there be discussions & work-  
shops? will there be quiet spaces? will  
people be able to get to the festival site  

by public transportation? what kinds  

of sensitivity & cultural awareness  
training will security people need?  
what about housing? medical care?  
outreach? scheduling? sound & light-  
ing equipment? insurance? licenses?  
police?

will artists' legal rights be protected?  
will artists' requests that their presen-  
tations not be videotaped/audiotaped/  
filmed/otherwise reproduced without  
prior written permission be honored?  
will media people be directed to ne-  
gotiate with artists to get permission  
to tape & film? will the festival nego-  
tiate media rights on the artists' behalf?  
will all of this take place before the  
festival starts so that there are no last  
minute surprises? will artists be  

prompt & pleasant & cooperative?

will the production staff be competent  
& calm? will there be any backstage  
tantrums? will the equipment act  
right?

will everybody who works get paid?  
is it possible to pay people what their  
labor is really worth? does all labor  
have an equal value? will there be a  
big gap in the fees paid to producers/  
artists/kitchen workers?

will the festival take any political  
stands?

will the festival issue a statement in  
support of all personal/political strug-  
gles against all types of oppression?  
will the festival issue a statement re-  
garding cigarette smoking, s & m, role  
playing, dildoes, nudity, frank language,  
pornography? if a woman is running  
for president will the festival issue a  
statement in support of her candidacy?  
will the festival refrain from issuing  
any political statements at all? will  
the festival encourage groups/individ-  
uals to circulate political materials?

what will happen when the glow wears  
off & we start getting on each other's  
nerves?

what about bruised sensibilities & feel-  
ings? what about ego tripping, career  
opportunism & idealistic starry-eyed-  
ness? what about age and size dif-  
ferences? class differences? religious  
differences? what about homophobia?  
what about heterophobia? what about  
differences around skin color, hair  
texture, lip & nose size? will wimmin  
who have enjoyed more power &  
privilege end up running everything?  
will the less privileged wimmin meekly  
or sullenly allow this to happen? will

there be space for discussions? will we  
talk about the oppression we suffer  
from the dominant racist patriarchal  
homophobic classist society? will we  
talk about neo-colonial self-hating  
oppression we inflict on ourselves &  
each other? will we get angry? cry?  
laugh? will there be resolution? will  
there be healing? will new alliances be  
formed? will there be reluctance to  
move beyond comfortable stances?

will you be there?

how about your parents? your kids?  
your girlfriend? your boyfriend?  
your lovers? will you get involved in  
the organizing? will you be an artist?  
will you lead a workshop? will you  
sell food or crafts? will you do day  
care? will you work in the office lick-  
ing stamps & answering phones? will  
you do fundraising? will you chop  
vegetables? will you watch the money?  
will you need someone to watch you  
watch the money? will you do con-  
struction work? tech? will you lay  
back & have a good time? after the  
festival is over & you are back home  
what will you remember?

what will we do with the profits?

will we need to set up a tax-exempt  
corporation before we get started?  
how will we find each other & come  
together in a representative organizing  
committee that has the skill/will to see  
this festival through? will we have a  
professional producer to take care of  
everything according to our direc-

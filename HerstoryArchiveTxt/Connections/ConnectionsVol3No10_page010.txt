GENERAL INFORMATION

Membership

The YWCA offers women of all ages the
opportunity to voice and act upon their
concerns.

As part of a local, national and international
organization operating in more than 80
countries, members of the Toledo YWCA are
members of the largest movement dedicated
to the needs of all women and girls, from

infants to senior citizens — all races, all

creeds, all economic levels.

The YWCA recognizes the urgent need for
women as women to come together for their
own personal growth and to assume responsi-
ble leadership in changing patterns to
improve the quality of life for all people.

Release your woman power at the Toledo
YWCA. Join today!

Members are entitled to participate in the
Toledo YWCA program at member fees,
register for classes, attend special
membership events and carry elected or
appointed leadership.

associateship/membership dues from each

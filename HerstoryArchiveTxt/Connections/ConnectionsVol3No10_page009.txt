Registration for the fall term begins  
Tuesday, September 1. No phone  
registrations can be accepted. Late registration  
will be accepted when possible, but no  
one will be allowed to enter classes after the  
second lesson. The fall term lasts eight weeks.  
Gym classes subject to minimum enrollments.

Current membership is required for all  
classes unless otherwise noted. You may be  
asked to present your membership card  
when you register. All registrations must be  
made in person or by mail, accompanied by  
full payment.

Refunds will be made only upon submission  
of a medical release or proof of moving out of  
town. The membership fee cannot be refunded.

Towel service is available for 25 cents. All  
participants must provide their own locks for  
lockers used during activities. All locks must  
be removed following each activity. The  
YWCA is not responsible for an individual's  
possessions.

national level. Information will be presented  
as follows:

• Disease concept  
• Identification of the problem  
• The family’s role  
• Treatment

Deadline for registration is Nov. 3. Call  
Sallie Popovec for more information.

All She Wrote is a feminist paper. By
this we mean the paper will:

1) Be supportive of all women,
2) Reach out to all women,

3) Provide a forum for input,
4) Be a growing process.

JOINING THE COLLECTIVE

Membership is open to any woman sharing
the group's goals and committed to the
collective process, which includes con-
sensus decision-making, non-hierarchical
structures, and sharing knowledge,
skills and power. The ASW Collective 
meets 2nd and 4th Tuesday at 7:30 pm
at the University YWCA. You need not be a
"writer" to join the collective. We
also need women to help plan, edit,
type, illustrate, proof, and paste up
each issue.

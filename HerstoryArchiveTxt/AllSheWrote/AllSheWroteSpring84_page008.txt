ALL SHE WROTE EDITORIAL POLICY

We encourage ideas, articles, and sup-
port from women everywhere. However, con-
trary to past policies, all articles are
subject to editing. If revisions seem to
be necessary the author will be given the
opportunity to rewrite their article. The
editorial staff will gladly work with all
writers.

University YWCA
Mission Statement

The University YWCA is a responsi-
ble, non-profit, voluntary association,
and a member of the National Student
YWCA which is responsive to the needs
and interests of women in the Univer-
sity community, including students,
faculty, and staff.

We uphold and advocate feminist val-
ues, cultural and social pluralism
and solidarity among women through par-
ticipation in alternative and unique
programs; by providing service,
education, information, and referrals,
and by operating as a clearinghouse on
women's issues for women in the univer-
sity community.

The one imperative is to thrust our
collective power toward the elimina-
tion of racism wherever it exists and
by any means necessary.

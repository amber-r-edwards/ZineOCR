This issue’s FOCUS section is on Asian American women.
The articles in this section reflect a diverse group of
Asian women, ages 20 to 65, American born and immigrants, 
workers, students and professionals. Specially featured in 
this section are three essays on Asian American Women’s 
Liberation by Miya Iwataki, Sasha Hohri and Sadie Lum 
who point to the need for a conscious and all-rounded 
solution to the problems Asian women face.

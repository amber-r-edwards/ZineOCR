Women Artists Wanted

The Woman's Building in Los Angeles
is seeking proposals for programming
in its new Gallery/Performing Space.
Women writers, performance/theater
artists, visual artists, musicians, and
lecturers are invited to submit pro-
posals for readings, exhibits, or per-
formances of their work. Proposals
should include a one-page description
of the project (e.g., space and equip-
ment needed, duration, dimensions),

a resume, and slides and/or other docu-

mentation of work. Proposals must be
submitted by January 1, 1984, for pro-
grams beginning April 1, 1984. Art-

ists will be notified by March 1, 1984.

Send proposals to: The Curatorial Com-
mittee, The Woman's Building, 1727

North Spring Street, Los Angeles, Cali-

fornia, 90012.

The Woman's Building, a public cen-
ter for women's culture, is celebrat—
ing its tenth year anniversary. The
newly renovated Gallery/Performance

Space is 1000 square feet, has theatri-

cal and track lighting, and a P.A. and
sound system.
Artists who are selected must as-

sume responsibility for most aspects of

installation and production of events.
At present time, the Woman's Building
is not funded to subsidize exhibitions
or events.

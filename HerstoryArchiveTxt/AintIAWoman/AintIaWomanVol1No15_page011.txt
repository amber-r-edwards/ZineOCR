of Bloomington, Indiana is sponsoring a  
Midwest abortion counselling conference for  
women now doing abortion counselling. Its

better prices and better places for women to  
go.  
Workshops planned are:  

Sexuality  

Special problems (women's parents)  

Counselling techniques  

Discussion with M.D. on saline method  

Abortion law repeal

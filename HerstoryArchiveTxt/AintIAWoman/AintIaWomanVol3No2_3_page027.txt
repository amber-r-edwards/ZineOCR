The fairy follies, (I still say it
means faggots when you say fairies, not
dykes.) came to Iowa City last weekend.
There were violin players, singers, har-
monica players, folk dancers, guitarists,
stand-up junkie comediennes, and one
outstanding bull whip-cracker. Not to
mention all the wandering children and
assorted roadies and stage hands.

The Iowa City Women's Community had
a local talent show for the benefit of
Sparky Daycare Center, charging a quart-
er a head. This was the second annual
event with the new added attraction of
no-men-allowed. (YEAH!) It was quite
well-attended and enjoyed by all, con-
sidering all the mistakes made by the
co-emcees, and the disappearing music
stand, thanks to 4-year-old Alice, (re-
member Alice?) And oh, yeah, false
starts and an un-coordinated lighting
system. I loved every minute of it!

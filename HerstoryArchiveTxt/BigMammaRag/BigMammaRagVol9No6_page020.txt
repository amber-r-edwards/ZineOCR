SELF DEFENSE. Many women
who are in prison for vio-
lent crimes are there be-
cause they tried to defend
themselves against attack-
ers. Self-defense law often
does not recognize these
attempts to stop wife bat-
terers or rapists. If you
have info on this problem
through personal experience,
or are an attorney who has
access to such briefs, con-
tact Kris Steinkoenig, 410
Hampshire College, Amherst,
MA 01002. Kris is doing a
thesis entitled, "A Politi-
cal Analysis of Self Defense
Law and its Application to
Women Who Kill in Self
Defense."

have had an abortion. Any-

ings or record them on
cassette tape and mail to:
P.O. Box 326, Harrisburg,
SD 57032. Your identity
isn't needed and you are
asked to omit it from your
correspondence. The state-
ments will be used at meet-

act misleading and exagger-
ated claims by so-called

Upcoming Issues of BMR

September

Non-theme issue.  
Deadline: August 16.

October

Women and Culture  
A look at how we are shaped by culture, and how we in turn can help shape our own culture.  
Suggested topics: reviews of women's art, music, literature; arts and crafts; feminist  
imagery in art; women's roles in film; culture, politics and money.

Deadline: September 18.

November

Women and Technology  
How women are affected by a highly technological society; what technology means in our  
lives and how we can learn to use it for ourselves.  
Suggested topics: interviews with women in technological fields; examination of how know-  
ledge of technology is controlled by men and used to oppress women; how women are taught  
to fear technology from an early age (like math and science in school); how technology  
has affected women's jobs.

Deadline: October 18.

December

Women and Dependencies  
An examination of what constitutes dependencies (drugs, alcohol, food, relationships) and  
how these affect women's lives.  
Suggested topics: how drugs are used by society to keep us disempowered (hospitals, mental  
institutions, prisons); how we use drugs and alcohol ourselves; when is a dependency healthy,  
or is that a contradiction in terms?; how relationships can become dependencies.

Deadline: November 20.

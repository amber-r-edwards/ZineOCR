Life Sober  

I am an alcoholic. For almost a  
year I have not had a drink. It has  
been extremely hard to stay sober at  
times, and I give myself a lot of  
credit for being strong enough to  
keep from drinking.  

But in the past year I have received  
very much support, encouragement,  
and love from my friends in LUNA. The  
understanding and concern I have re-  
ceived from these women has shown me  
that I am not alone in my struggles.  
We have helped each other through  
many crises. These women are my  
friends, and they care about what  
happens to me.  

There is comfort from a friend's  
hug that wine has never given me.  
And I have learned that being loved,  
understood and cared about does a  
lot more for my self-concept than  
a six-pack of Millers.  

I put away the wine. Tonight I  
will call my friends from LUNA. They  
will reassure me that I am a good  
person and that they are sorry that  
I am sad. Life will probably still be  
shitty tomorrow. But at least I know  
that I am not alone, and that I will  
probably deal with it sober.

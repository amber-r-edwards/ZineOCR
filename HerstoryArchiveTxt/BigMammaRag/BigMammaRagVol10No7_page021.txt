Core group members are required to:

1. Go through a 3 month training and orientation process.

2. Make a written commitment which will include a list of her projects/responsibilities and the regular weekly hours she will be at the library.

3. Agree to work on the goals set by the DFLC.

4. Know all aspects related to the maintenance and functioning of the library.

5. Attend required meetings.

6. Be involved in the decision making process.

The support groups consist of women interested in helping to create and maintain a feminist library. They assist the cooperative with all aspects of the library.  
Support group members are required to:

1. Meet with the DFLC to discuss their interest and proposed activities.

2. Attend meetings, as needed, to share with the DFLC their progress, questions, concerns and suggestions.

3. Complete one-time projects or ongoing tasks.

HOW CAN I BECOME A PART OF THE DENVER FEMINIST LIBRARY

I CAN DO SO ON MANY DIFFERENT LEVELS:

You can become a member of the core or support groups. The core group meets every Tuesday, drop by and meet with us. Call 623-8191 or 393-0798 for location.

You can donate books, periodicals, money, time or ideas. Boxes are located at 835 Lipan and 1547 High Street for books and periodicals. Ideas can be phoned or mailed (address on back). Donations can be mailed or delivered to the addresses listed above.

You can tell other women you know about the DFL.

You can use the books and periodicals in the DFL (opening date is expected for early fall 1982 in the Capital Hill area).

You can use the DFL to share information about various activities of interest to women in this area.

You can attend the "BUILD A LIBRARY" PARTY. Four books will be charged as admission into this event. If you do not have any books which pertain to or will be of use to women, a cash donation will be accepted. Come and meet with us on the DFLC!

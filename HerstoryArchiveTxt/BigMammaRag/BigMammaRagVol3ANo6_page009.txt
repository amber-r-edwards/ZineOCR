Big Mama Rag Vol. 3-A. No. 6

From All of Us to Both of You

Photos by Kate Sharp

For about a year now two incredibly dynamic and beautiful women
have blessed the life of Big Mama Rag and the lives of those closely
involved with her. Their names are Linda Fowler and Carol Lease and
for a year they have acted as the paper's coordinators. For the next few
months they will be physically separated from the rest of us working
here and we would like to take this opportunity to thank them for their
unceasing and dauntless energies and to wish them well. Never before
have we been able to do this in print because never before could we
have pulled it off without their prior knowledge.

Big Mama Rag has dedicated many lines of print to women highly
active in the movement. But usually those words describe the actions of
women in the spotlight and in the news. There are thousands of
women who constantly dedicate incredible energy and work hours to
feminism (as much as do the more well-known women) who never
seem to get properly thanked. So as we at Big Mama mourn our
temporary loss, we would like to make just such a declaration of thanks.

Carol and Linda have been an important part of our evolution, have

Big Mama Rag would like to thank these two well-beloved sisters for

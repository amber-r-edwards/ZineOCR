"Meeting of Equals", a peer support group for rape  
victims, will begin meeting in late March. If you  
have been sexually assaulted, get together with  
other women who have, also, to talk and support  
each other. Send last name and way to contact to:  
Meeting of Equals, P.O. Box 10741, Denver, 80210.  

The Twelfth Annual International Conference for Human  
Rights and Against Psychiatric Oppression will be held  
in Pueblo, Colorado June 1-6 1984. Pueblo is the "home"  

of the Colorado State Hospital for the Insane, and is  
not very far from Canon City, CO where the state's adult  
prisons are located. The prisons are known for their  
abusive behavior modification programs and uninformed and  
forced drugging. The conference is a gathering of cur-  
rent/ex-psychiatric inmates/"mental patients" from across  
the US and Canada. Anti-psychiatry activists, (who are  
not ex-inmates themselves) are welcome if sponsored by  
an ex-inmate group. We gather together to support each  
other, to together join in ending psychiatric oppres-  
sion. Ex-inmates living in the Denver/Pueblo area who  
can work on the conference, and for anyone who wants  
more info, contact us by writing: 12th Annual Conf. Comm  

Committee, Box 61307, Denver, CO 80206;

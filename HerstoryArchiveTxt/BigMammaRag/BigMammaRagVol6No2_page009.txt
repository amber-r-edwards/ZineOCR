International Women's Week will be celebrated  
MARCH 4th-11th at the University of Colorado, in  
Boulder. Sponsored by the Women's Liberation Coali-  
tion and the Lesbian Caucus. There will be speakers  
on numerous issues including: Women's Herstory, Wom-  
en and Aging, Self-Defense, Women in the Far East,  
the Double Oppression of Third World Women, Issues  
and Topics on Socialist Feminism, Psychology and the  
Media, Women and Humor, Assertiveness Training, Self  
Help, Domestic Relations, Discrimination in Housing,  
Schools and Education, Women in Constitutional Is-  
sues, Lesbian Rights, and more! Some of the other  
events will include ‘big name' women's music, poets,  
artists, films, a coffee-house, and a pot-luck din-  
ner to round off the women's week. All events will  
take place on the CU campus or nearby Boulder area.  
Call 492-7735 for more information.

A meeting to implement the Relationships Task Force  
of the Colorado Plan of Action passed at the Boulder  
Conference last June will be held FEBRUARY 25th.  
Mandates from last year include the decriminalization  
of prostitution, passage of a lesbian rights law,  
legal safeguards for battered women, reforms in sin-  
gle parent laws and social security, enforcement of  
child support and custody laws, and the formulation  
of laws to deal with violence against women as typi-  
fied in pornography.

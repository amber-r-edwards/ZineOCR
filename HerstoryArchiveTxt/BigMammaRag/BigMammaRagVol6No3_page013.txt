SATURDAY, March 4

Rally for International Women's Day in  
Denver. The IWD coalition is sponsoring

Church, 14th and Lafayette. (See  

story this issue).

MONDAY, March 6

International Women's Week begins today  
at the University of Colorado in  
Boulder, where dozens of workshops,  
films, panels and discussions will  
take place. Today's schedule is as  
follows: 11 am: Double oppression of  
Chicana women, UMC 157A; noon, Sex  
discrimination in schools, housing  

and employment, Room 157B; 1 pm, Me  
and my friends—a feminist art show,  
Room 157A; 2-3 pm, Self-defense demon-  
stration, Room 305; 3-4 pm, Rape cul-  
ture film, Room 305; 8:30-10 pm,  
“Womanhouse" and “Woman," two films  

in Room 305.

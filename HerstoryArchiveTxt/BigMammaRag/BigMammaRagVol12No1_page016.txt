In last month's Where We're At, we "announced" that Sue
Goding and Joan Pettijohn were pregnant. We said they "got
artificially inseminated". Well really that's not quite
what happened. Since they are really pregnant--it was hard-
ly "artificial". Also, the way it was written sounds like
Joan and Sue were totally passive in the situation--like
they were waiting on a street corner and some sperm leapt
out at them...Hardly!

What was involved was a whole lot of political arguments,
struggle and fights between a few women who organized to
collect sperm from different men. The politics of who con-
trols reproduction--maintaining male power was always at
issue...among lesbian feminists in the community as well as
with the men. The politics of the family, who the "real
father" is, fatherhood, and male ownership of children are
really complicated issues. It was/is really political work.

Well, we think that about covers our "stuff" for this
month. Cover your ass in the New Year!

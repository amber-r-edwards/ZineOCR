Third World Women's Archives:
to further the development of
a network of information and
support for 3rd World Women
in the U.S. and abroad. The
principal goal is to collect,
preserve, and circulate
multi-lingual materials,
including personal papers,
letters, diaries, photos,
tapes, artwork, sheet music,
etc. Send to 3rd World Wom-
en's Archives, 453 Edgewood,
New Haven, CT 06511.

A FILM COMPETITION co-sponsored by
Grass-Roots Television and the Aspen
Foundation for the Arts for "Colorado
Film Makers Only" is currently accept-
ing films. Films can be short stories,
documentaries, animations, sports, etc.,
and should run 5 to 15 minutes in
length. 16mm preferred, but 1/2 and 3/4
inch video tape or Super 8 is accept-
able - either black and white, or color.
New films, or films produced after Jan 1, 1976 are eligible.

Awards will be given to the best film
in two categories; professional, and
films made by people not primarily en-
gaged in the film business. Numerous
prizes will be awarded and substantial
publicity state-wise and nationally is
assured. Films must be received in
Aspen by June 1st.

writing about, dreaming about, and try-
ing to form feminist social change com-
munities, I'd like to correspond with
you. We can share our visions, hopes,
strategies, anxieties. Send me let-
ters, material, proposals, scattered
thoughts, and I'll respond with some
of the ideas and proposals I've been
formulating for some time. I would
also like to exchange your ideas with
other women who write. Write me: Beane
at PO Box 405-P, Rt. 2, Hillsborough
N.C., 27278. I'll reply immediately.

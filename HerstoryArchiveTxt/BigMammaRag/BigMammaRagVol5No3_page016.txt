FEMINIST CALENDAR

March 31 and April 1, Thursday and Friday-- Colorado Women's College, Houston Fine Arts Center. Conference on Battered Women. This two-day workshop is aimed

at community workers dealing with battered women. The general public is welcome as well. Erin Pizzey of London will be keynote speaker. The program also includes films, reactor panel, discussion groups. 8:30 am - 4:00 pm. $30.

April 3, Sunday-- April Fools show on Women Everywhere. 2:00 pm - 2:30 pm. KFML Radio, 1390 AM. (Every Sunday at 2:00)

April 7, Thursday-- 1st Annual Auraria Women's Celebration. 10:00 am - 9:00 pm, Open to everyone. Movies, workshops, entertainment, theatre, art displays of women students, raffle. Free. Children welcome. Sponsored by the Associated Women Students of Metro and the CCD and UCD Women's Centers. At the Auraria Campus.

April 7, Thursday-- An open meeting intent upon organizing a women's health clinic in Denver. 7:00 pm at Woman to Woman Feminist Center, 2023 E. Colfax. Call Sue, 837-0549 or Mary, 322-1664 for more info.

April 8, Friday-- Colorado Women's College, Porter Library. Assertiveness Training for Women- A psychological tune-up for the woman who wants to make better choices for herself. Enrollment limited. Childcare available. $25.

April 16, Saturday-- First Aid Class at Woman to Woman. 9:30 am - 6:00 pm. This class, upon successful completion will authorize you to carry a first aid card. Bring sack lunch. Free childcare. Call 837-0549 for more info.

April 17, Sunday-- A potluck picnic for women and children at Cheesman Park in Denver. Assemble at the 12th Ave side of the park at 11:00 am. Bring frisbees, softballs, etc.

April 18, Monday-- Big Mama Rag Critique meeting at Woman to Woman. 7:00 pm. All women are encouraged to come to this meeting to express her feelings on the Jan/Feb, March and April issues of the Paper.

April 22 and 23, Friday and Saturday-- The 1977 Chicana Conference at the University of Colorado in Boulder.

Lots of events, including the presentation of the Mariposa Award to a woman who has contributed significantly to the Chicana and Chicano movements. For more information call Irene Blea Gutierrez, 442-7669 or Virginia Maestas, 499-9359.

April 22 - 24, Friday through Sunday-- the 1977 National Conference of the National Organization for Women in Detroit, Michigan. Call Fran, 755-8790 or Donna, 837-9289 for more info.

April 29, Friday-- A Women's Film Festival at the University of Colorado at Colorado Springs. Call Susan Mulacek at UCCS for more info.

Be sure to have your group send next month's calendar items to Big Mama Rag by April 20th.

COORDINATOR POSITION OPEN. Denver, CO-- Many groups and individuals from the gay community of Colorado have come together over the past year to establish a gay community center in Denver. Activities such as weekend retreats and successful fundraisers have indicated a wide base of support. To date an interim board of directors and six working committees are functioning. Although many activities will be determined by the people who use the center, some programs will include: counseling, referrals, meeting space, a library, a coffee house and a variety of educational, social and cultural activities.

The interim board of directors is now accepting applications for a full-time salaried coordinator for the center. Send resumes or inquiries to the following address by April 30, 1977. The Gay Community Center, PO Box 2024, Denver, CO 80201. Attn: CSC.

Rita Silk  

Rita Silk (Nauni) is a  
Lakota Sioux woman who was  
convicted in Oklahoma of  
first degree manslaughter  
and attempted murder. While  
attempting to defend her son  
from assault by 2 police, she  
shot and killed one and  
wounded the other. She was  
sentenced to 150 years by her  
trial judge, Cannon, who  
stated, "If it were up to me,  
I'd give her more." Judge  
Cannon has been disqualified  
from the case for violating  
the judicial Canon of Ethics.  
The defense has moved for a  
new trial on the basis of 36  
errors in the first trial.  
Rita was released from prison  
on $100,000 bond on April 30,  
awaiting her appeal.

being labeled a "convicted

ginning of September. She is

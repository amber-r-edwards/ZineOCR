A little over two years ago,

a dozen representatives from
various progressive political
groups met with a few Eritrean
students to learn about the
Eritrean struggle for national
liberation. This group gradu-

ally grew into the Eritrean
Support Committee of Colorado.
The support committee has
frequently held fundraising
dinners, participated with
other groups to present events
promoting national liberation
struggles, published a quart-
erly newsletter, Eritrea in
Revolution, and most recently
is giving a seminar series
through Denver Free University.

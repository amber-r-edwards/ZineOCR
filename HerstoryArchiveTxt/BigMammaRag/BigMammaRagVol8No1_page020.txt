WORKING FOR YOUR LIFE is an hour-
length color film focusing on the
hazards faced by today's 43 million
American working women. It is the only
documentary film about the health

and safety of women on the job.
Filmed in 40 different workplaces,
Working For Your Life highlights

both jobs traditionally held by women
and jobs into which women are just
beginning to move. Over a sound track
of blues and country music, women

speak candidly about the problems they

face at work. To rent contact: LOHP
Films, Transit Media, 779 Susquehanna
Ave, Franklin Lakes, NJ 07417.

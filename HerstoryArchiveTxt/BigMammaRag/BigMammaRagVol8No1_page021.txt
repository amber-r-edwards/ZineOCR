Upcoming Issues of BMR

March

International Women's Issue  
Articles about women's involvement in liberation movements all over  
the world; how various religions affect women; how American Imperial-  
ism affects women world-wide; how do we build international solidari-  
ty between women; how can American women support other revolutionary  

struggles?  
deadline: February 14  

April

Women in Institutions  
Articles from and about women who are or have been in prison, mental  
institutions, nursing homes, etc.; information from support groups  
and defense organizations; personal experiences around incarceration;  
why are women locked up?  

deadline: March 18  

May

Non-theme issue.  
deadline: April 17  

June

Class  
Articles addressing the question is the women's movement a white,  
middle class movement; how does classism express itself in the wo-  
men's community; how does it divide us, and around what issues; what  
roles do academic feminists and professional women play in the move-  
ment; how do we as feminists address the survival issues of all women?  

deadline: May 15

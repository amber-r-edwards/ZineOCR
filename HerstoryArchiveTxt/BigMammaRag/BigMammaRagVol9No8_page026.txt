THE BOSTON WOMEN'S HEALTH BOOK
COLLECTIVE has just begun a
major revision of Our Bodies,
Ourselves. In addition to sec-
tions on reproductive issues,
taking care of ourselves,
sexuality and relationships,
violence against women, and
health and medical care, they
plan to add sections about
women in the workplace, en-
vironmental hazards, repro-
ductive technology, alter-
native forms of health care,
disabilities, midlife and
growing older. They will
be making stronger distinc-
tions between health care
and medical care. Throughout
the book they want to in-
clude more experiences from
women of color, women with
older women. Your written
contributions will shape
the book. Send poems or jour-
nal entries, etc. to BWHBC,
Box 192, West Somerville, MA
02144.

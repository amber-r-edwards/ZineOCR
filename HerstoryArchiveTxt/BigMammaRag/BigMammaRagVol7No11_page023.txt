National  

February, 1980 New York City: Second Third World Lesbian Writers Conference  
sponsored by the AZALEA Collective. Contact Joan Gibbs, 306 Lafayette  
Ave., Brooklyn, New York 11238.  

February 27-March 2 San Francisco: 11th National Convention on Women and the  
Law. All people who have expertise in areas related to women and the  
law should contact Nancy Polikoff at (202) 783-3410 to discuss being  
panelists at the convention. For other information on the conference  
contact the Women's Legal Defense Fund, 1010 Vermont Avenue, N.W.  
Washington, D.C. 20005. (202) 638-1123.  

March-July Far West Laboratory for Educational Research and Development is  
planning a series of two-day workshops in cities around the country  
(tentative list of locations includes Colorado Springs, CO). The

educational equity projects. Contact: Estrella Fichter-Ruiz,  
Far West Laboratory, 1855 Folsom Street, San Francisco, CA 94103,  
(415) 565-3173.

Hannah Lerman, 1980 AWP Conference Coordinator, 1543 South Oakhurst  
Drive, Los Angeles, CA 90035, (213) 277-4948.

(713) 488-9326.  

May 16-20 Bloomington, Indiana: National Women's Studies Association--

Coordinator, NWSA, University of Maryland, College Park, MD 20742.

WOMEN WHO ARE BLIND OR PHYS-
ICALLY DISABLED can receive
feminist and lesbian books,
periodicals, and other inform-
ation on women's issues from
the Women's Braille Press.
WBP offers materials on four
track cassette and in Braille
on both a loan and purchase
basis. WBP also produces a
newsletter in Braille, print
and tape. For a sample news-
letter and more info contact
Women's Braille Press, PO Box
8475, Mpls., MN 55408. Volun-
teer readers and contributors
are also needed to record
feminist and lesbian litera-
ture on cassette tape.

WOMEN OPPOSED TO NUCLEAR
TECHNOLOGY (WONT) is a fem-
inist women's anti-nuclear
organization based in Santa
Cruz, California. They are
calling on other feminists to
join them in a planned direct
action against the Diablo
Canyon Nuclear Power Plant
located between Santa Cruz
and Los Angeles. The exact
date has not been set; at
that time a nationwide alert
will begin. If you are in-
terested, or need more info,
write: WONT, 1516 Broadway,
#2, Santa Cruz, CA 95062.
Phone (408) 427-0176.

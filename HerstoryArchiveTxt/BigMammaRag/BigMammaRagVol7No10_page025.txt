Demands of Women

demand free, adequate health care  
demand free, 24 hour day care  
demand the right to the use of self-defense  
demand equality in the job market  
demand the right to guaranteed jobs or income  
demand free and relevant education  
demand an end to sexism in the cultural media  
demand an end to the oppression of lesbians  

demand an end to privilege based on race  
and class  

demand the end of defining people in terms of  
their relationships to others  

-We demand the right of self-determination for  
all people  
demand the right to use any means necessary  
to eliminate male supremacy

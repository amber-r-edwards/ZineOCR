Washington Street #403. Main topic will be employment discrimination because
of sex.

SMALL BUSINESS MANAGEMENT workshop, sponsored by the Colorado Feminist Federal
Credit Union at St. Thomas Church, 23rd and Dexter. 8 pm. Free.

WAPA (Women Against Psychiatric Assault) will meet every other Wednesday at
Woman to Woman Feminist Bookcenter, 2023 E. Colfax, Denver at 6:30 pm.

at CU Boulder, UMC 157B at 4 pm. CU Women's studies.

EVERYWOMAN'S PLACE,  
Burlington’s new women’s center,  
will hold three events in celebration  
of this week:  

POTLUCK LUNCH, Sunday,  
March 8, 11 a.m. — 2 p.m., at Every-  
woman's Place, 200 Main St., Bur-  
lington (second floor).  

OLD NEWSREELS, Wednesday,  
March 11, 8 p.m., at Everywoman’s  
Place. Subjects include People’s Park  
in Berkeley; the Yippies at the 1968  
Democratic Convention; the Ver-  

mont Free Farm (Putney, 1970); and  
Elizabeth Cotton, who wrote  
“Freight Train” and other songs.  
Admission will be $2, and that  
includes popcorn.  

HOLLY NEAR in concert,  
Saturday, March 14, 8 p.m., at the  
Flynn Theater, Burlington. Tickets  
are available at Bailey’s Music,  
Burlington, and at Everywoman’s  
Place at $5.50 and $6.50; tickets will  
be $6 and $7 at the door. Childcare  
provided; call Joan Hockey, 658-  
4617. Co-sponsored by Every-  
woman's Place and VPIRG.  

For more information on any of  
these events, call Everywoman’s Place  
at 658-2422.

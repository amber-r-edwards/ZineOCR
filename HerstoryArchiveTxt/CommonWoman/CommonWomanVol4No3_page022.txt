WRITE FOR US!

Commonwoman wants YOU to write for us. We want to
broaden our appeal and reach more women, and we can't do that
without your help. We need your articles, poems, short stories
and letters. Pick any topic you think would interest women. 
If you need help, here are a few suggestions to get you started:

Women’s Sports (including news about the Women’s Soft-
ball League, canoeing, hiking, camping, rock climbing, etc.)

Older Women (40 and up; this is the theme of our next issue)

My Philosophy of... (Life, Work, Housework, Marriage, etc.)

News Items for News Briefs and Atrocities

Reports on Women’s Events Around the State

Feminist Analyses (What is feminism to you? How do your
feminist politics affect your views of capitalism, war, child-rear-
ing, marriage, relationships, etc.?)

Personal Stories (“Why I Chose to Marry/Not to Marry,”
“Why I Chose to Have Children/Not to Have Children,” “Living
as a Lesbian in Vermont,” etc.)

Interviews with Interesting Women

Book Reviews, Movie Reviews, Play and Concert Reviews.
We also want your photographs, cartoons and graphics —
you pick your topics.

05404. Our deadline for the next issue is May 10.

We’re counting on hearing from you!

MINNESOTA WOMEN'S POLITICAL CAUCUS (MWPC)

This 3 1/2 year-old group works to place more  
women in public office and to make women more  
effective in the political arena. This summer  
the organization will be involved in fund-  
raising activities. It will hold a Seneca  
Falls Festival, July 14, tickets $5.00. The  
group is also planning a tribute to State Rep.  
Helen McMillan for the near future. MWPC's  
minority caucus is very active. This summer  
it will be completing "census tracts" to dis-  
cover where the largest concentration of minorit-  
ies are, who is running for office, and who  
the organization should support for office. The  
minority caucus also plans to bring the U.S.'  
three black women mayors to the Twin Cities  
following this fall's primary elections. A  
conference will be planned around this event.  
The National Women's Political Caucus annual  
conference will be June 28-30 in Wichita, Kan-  
sas. MWPC will send delegates.

For information on any of the group's activi-  
ties, call: 225-0660 or 225-8194, or write:

P.O. Box 146  
St. Paul, Minn. 55102

Annual dues are $15.00 and cover local, state  
and national dues. Scholarship plans are  
available for those who cannot pay $15.00.

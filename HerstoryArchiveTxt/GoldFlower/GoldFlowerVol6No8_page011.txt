Community Education Courses for Women

Several courses designed to help women become more  
open and more self-aware will be offered this spring  
in Southeast Minneapolis. These include Assertiveness  
Training for Women; Protecting Yourself Against  
Rape; Body Work/Bioenergetics for Women; Belly Dancing;  
Journal Keeping; and Sexism: How it Works.

Most courses cost less than $10.00.

Daytime and evening classes begin the week of April  
14th; registration begins March 17th. For more  
information and brochure of course descriptions call  
376-4505 or stop in at the Community Education Office  
at 411 University Avenue S. E. weekdays.

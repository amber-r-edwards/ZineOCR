CONFERENCE INFORMATION

NATIONAL  
ORGANIZATION  
FOR WOMEN  

Registration  

Registration for the Conference will be  
held at the Benjamin Franklin Hotel in the  
Garden Terrace. For the convenience of  
early arrivals, registration will begin at 5:00  
PM on Thursday, October 23, 1975 and  
continue until 11:00 PM. Registration will  
reopen on Friday, October 24 at 9:00 AM  
and close at 11:00 PM. On Saturday,  
registration will be conducted from 8:00  
AM to 11:00 AM at the Benjamin Franklin  
Hotel and thereafter at the Civic Center  
(site of the plenary sessions and elections  
of officers).  

ALL MEMBERS ARE URGED TO BRING  
PROOF OF MEMBERSHIP WITH THEM,  
i.e. 1975 MEMBERSHIP CARD, CAN-  
CELLED CHECK, OR OTHER PROOF.  
Even members of long standing should  
bring these credentials to ensure that there  
will be no problem with the registration  
process. Credentialing of members will be  
conducted by the American Arbitration  
Association.  

Persons who pre-register and whose mem-  
bership has been recorded will find their  
full credentials, a ticket to the special event  
and their badge waiting for them. PRE-  
REGISTER, it will make everything easier.  

Display and Fund Raising Tables  
for the 1975 National Conference.  
Philadelphia, October 24-27, are  
now available for $25 for a 5’ table

Transportation  

Prior issues of DO IT NOW contained the  
full details of travel information to Phila-  
delphia. In addition, chapter presidents,  
state coordinators, and regional direc-  
tors have received copies of this in-  
formation. For those arriving via these  
planes or buses, transportation to both  
hotels is provided. For those arriving by  
other modes of transportation, the  
following information may be useful:  

The Benjamin Franklin Hotel is located at  
9th and Chestnut Streets.  

The Bellevue Stratford Hotel is located at  
Broad and Walnut Streets (Broad Street  
exists in lieu of 14th Street).  

Limousine service at a  
cost of $2.80 is always  
available; limousines leave  
about every 15 min-  
utes and go directly to  
the hotels. Taxis are  
also available, cost is  
about $8.00. But from  
6:15 A.M. until 10:45  
P.M., there is a bus,  
every half-hour on the  
quarter hour, at a cost of  
$1 per person. It stops  
at 13th and Market, and  
at 8th and Market (Mar-  
ket is one block north of  
Chestnut and 3 blocks  
north of Walnut).  

From the airport:  

Cabs are available; they  
cost about $3.00. The  
“L” also stops at 30th  
Street Station; for 352,  
it will take you to 15th  
and Market, and on to  
8th and Market. Buses  
are also available on  
30th Street; they make  
the same stops as the  
‘L’ does.  

From the trains:  

From the buses: Trailways is located at  
13th and Arch; Grey-  
hound at 17th and Mar-  
ket. From the latter,  
buses go on Market  
Street. From the former,  
Arch is two blocks north  
of Market where buses  
can be found. With min-  
imal luggage, both are  
within a 15-minute walk  
of either hotel, using the  
longest distance between  
two points.  

Child Care  

If you wish to use the child care facilities at  
the conference, please register early,  
noting the ages and number of children you  
are bringing. Child care will be provided  
at all times that there are conference ac-  
tivities. In addition, all children are invited  
to the conference itself, especially to the  
opening session at Independence Mall and  
to the ERA Action on Monday. All con-  
ference participants are invited to spend  
time at the child care rooms, which will be  
located in both hotels. In order to help in  
planning to meet the needs of tomorrow's  
feminists, please register NOW.

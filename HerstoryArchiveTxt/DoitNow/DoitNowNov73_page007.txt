DO YOU HAVE FEMINIST SLIDE PRESENTATIONS OR FILMS??

to hear from NOW chapters which have prepared slide presentations or films on feminist topics. A

description of content to: NOW Public Information Office, 641 Lexington Avenue, Sixth Floor,  
New York, N. Y. 10022.

ATTENTION LOST NOW MEMBERS!  

The NOW National Office has issued a plea — “Our lost members file is growing. Please notify  

us when you change your address. When mail is returned, we contact the closest chapter, and  

if we cannot get a new address from them, your name goes in the ‘lost file.’ There are almost  

300 1973 members in that file now. Please let us know about four weeks ahead if you are  
moving and where.” NOW NATIONAL OFFICE, 1957 East 73rd Street, Chicago, Illinois 60649.

NOW LDEF PUBLIC SERVICE AD CAMPAIGN BREAKS $3 MILLION MARK!

NOW in its precedent and record-breaking seventh month, the NOW LDEF Public Service Ad  
Campaign has reached a media value of over 3 million dollars. The ads have appeared on  
some 249 radio stations in 48 states, 262 television stations in 47 states, approximately 36  
public broadcast stations and even the BBC.

Print ads have been published in many national magazines and other print media, with a value  
of almost $500,000. The magazines include Time, Newsweek, Saturday Review/World,  
Nation’s Business, MS., The New Yorker, Sports Illustrated and many others.

The response from the advertising world continues to be highly favorable as is the reaction  
from the general public. Letters still swamp the NOW LDEF Office in New York, reports  
Ad Campaign Coordinator Midge Kovacs, with over 97% of them favorable.
